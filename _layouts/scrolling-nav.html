Conservative Americans Club

Next meeting is Thursday, February 22, 2018 at 6:30pm. We are located at Family Pharmacy Corporate Office, 4101 N. Highway NN - Ozark, MO 65721,

Featuring guest speaker Paul Curtman to discuss The Proper Role of Government - As articulated in the Declaration of Independence, Natural Rights, and State Sovereignty.

Representative Curtman is Chairman of the House Conservative Caucus and Chair of the Committee on Ways and Means. We are delighted to have him come and address this important subject. Paul is also a candidate for Missouri State Auditor. Please mark your calendar and plan to come and meet Rep. Curtman and hear him speak.

The Conservative Americans Club has plans to host The Making of America Seminar on Saturday, March 24, 2018. This is an excellent all-day seminar on the Constitution, put on by the National Center for Constitutional Studies. The cost is $25 per adult and $15 per student; and includes lunch. This Making of America Seminar is open to all. Invite your friends, children, and grandchildren to sign up and come.

The CAC welcomes back Calvin Morrow as its guest speaker, Thursday, April 19, 2018.
